Dubai Marina

Dubai Marina is one of the world's largest and most luxurious waterfront residential communities developed by Emaar. Dubai Marina suggests a vast majority of apartments for rent to its visitors.

Jumeirah Village Circle

Located in the vibrant heart of new Dubai, this residence provides all the amenities needed for a luxurious lifestyle in a peaceful environment. Positioned between Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Road and Al Khail Road, JVC.

Port De la Mer

Dubai's premier beachfront destination, featuring shopping, dining, and entertainment for the whole family. Enjoy 2.5 kilometers of pristine white sand and a modern, minimalist design. It's the ultimate beach experience in Jumeira 1.

Business Bay

Business Bay is one of the trendy residential and professional hubs in Dubai. Set amidst the gleaming waters of the Dubai Canal, it is neighbours with Dubai Mall and Burj Khalifa.

Palm Jumeirah

Palm Jumeirah is renowned for luxury hotels, fine dining, and a scenic boardwalk offering views of Dubai's coast. Beach clubs become lively nightclubs with DJs after dark.

IMPZ

Dubai Production City, formerly the International Media Production Zone, is a hub for media production companies in Dubai, located in Me'aisem.
